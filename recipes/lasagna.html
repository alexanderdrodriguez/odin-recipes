Skillet Lasagna

This no-bake skillet lasagna is made right on your stovetop and is a fast and easy alternative to store-bought hamburger mixes!

Ingredients

  • Half a pound of ground beef
  • A jar of spaghetti sauce
  • Chopped Onions

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat for 7 minutes. And add all of the ingredients, cook over low heat until sauce is hot, about 15 minutes.
  2. Boil water in another pot for about 8 minutes, then drain..
  3. Add cooked and drained noodles to the sauce and stir until completely coated.

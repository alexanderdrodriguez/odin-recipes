Homemade Pepproni Pizza

An easy yet tasty Pepporoni Pizza anyone can make from Home!

Ingredients

  • Tomato paste
  • Pizza crust
  • American Cheese

Steps

  1. Knead flour for about 4 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees, and top crusts with sauce, and cheese.
  3. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes until crusts are browned and cheese is bubbly.
